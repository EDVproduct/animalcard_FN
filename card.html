HONEY BEE

There are 16,000 species of bees. Most are solitary insects; only about 5 percent are social bees, the most common being the honeybee. As many as 80,000 of them colonize a single hive.

  • scientificname: Apis
  • Habitat: Worldwide
  • Current population trend: Unknown
  • Size : 0.4 to 0.6 in (workers)
  • Diet: Herbivores

The honeybee is the best known bee species because of the general appearance and the large numbers of specimens. The honeybee is appreciated by humans because of the important role as pollinator of many plant species such as fruit trees. In addition, the honeybee is the most important supplier of various natural products such as honey, beeswax, royal jelly and propolis.
